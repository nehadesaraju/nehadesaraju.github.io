Background

I’m a high school senior based in Dallas, TX and I’m the entertainment editor of The Sidekick, my high school’s award-winning student-run online and print publication. I’m also the founder and editor-in-chief of Jacquerie Magazine, a publication exploring the intersection between power and people, by and for young people. I have words in Mic and Keke Magazine am a fellow with Solomon Rajput's Congressional campaign. Previously, I was the national communications director of U.S. Youth Climate Strike.

I have a huge passion for data science, innovation, and creative arts, especially where they meet social justice. My freelancing is focused on politics and Gen Z culture, but my interests are varied—from science and math to journalism, design, and video production. Recently, I’ve been thinking about how data- and science-driven public policy and city planning can bring about a better world.

When I'm not in front of a computer screen, I’m probably singing, making art, or spending time with my two little sisters (sometimes all three).
